The Land of Splendour and Heritage

Discover the rich history, vibrant culture, and breathtaking natural beauty of Karnataka. From ancient temples and royal palaces to lush green hills and pristine beaches, there's a world to explore.

Popular Tourist Destinations

Mysore

Known as the "City of Palaces," Mysore is a hub of art and culture. The majestic Mysore Palace is a must-visit, especially during the Dasara festival.

Learn More

Hampi

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Hampi is an open-air museum of ruins from the Vijayanagara Empire. The stunning stone architecture will transport you back in time.

Learn More

Coorg (Kodagu)

Often called the "Scotland of India," Coorg is a picturesque hill station famous for its coffee plantations, misty mountains, and lush landscapes.
